Modes

From the side menu  you can select different modes according to the number of players
There are 2, 3 or 4 players mode.

Scorekeeper

Points Buraco is a scorekeeper
Choose the right mode and start playing Buraco
After an hand, insert the score of each player. A white field is considered zero and you can write scores in the order you prefer
When you finished entering the scores, touch  and totals will be automatically updated.
Continue to play, until one of the players wins. The app will alert you in case of victory
Game finishes at 2005 Points, but you can customize it in Settings

Share results

You can share your scores on Facebook, Whatsapp, Instagram, Telegram and other apps.
Just press  to share your current match with friends
TIP: add the hashtag #PointsBuraco to your post.

Change names

It is really easy: touch a name to edit it.
To revert to default names, touch  and select "Restore default names"

Change photos

Press  to add a photo from your Gallery
TIP: you can enable profile pics in Settings
